Eggless Banana Walnut ChocolateChip Cake

Description

Eggless Banana Walnut ChocolateChip cake will have a nice aroma of cinnamon flavor. If you are a cinnamon lover, you will definitely love this recipe.

Now let's dive into the ingredients that are needed for this amazing and wonderful eggless banana walnut chocolate chip cake.

Ingredients

  • 4 ripened bananas
  • 3 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 tsp of vanilla essence
  • 1 tsp of baking powder
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp of cinnamon powder
  • a pinch of salt
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup of chopped walnuts
  • 1/2 cup of chocolate chips
  • 1 -- 9x13 baking pan

Steps

  1. In a bowl add the 4 ripened bananas and smash it with fork very well
  2. To this add brown sugar, vegetable oil and beat it well until it is smooth
  3. Add vanilla essence and milk and mix thoroughly
  4. Now add all the dry ingredients: all purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon powder and salt to the mixture
  5. Mix well and there should not be any lumps
  6. If the mixture is too tight, you can add 2 tbsp of milk
  7. Time to add walnuts and chocolate chip and fold gently
  8. Pour it on the greased pan and bake it in 350 F for 25-30 mins
  9. Your cake is ready!
